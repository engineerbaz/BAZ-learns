# FinOps
Collaboration of Technical, Finance, and Business to cater billing/cost of different products and vendors


## FinOps Framework
It enables organizations to **maximize the business value of cloud and Technology, Enable Timely data-driven decision-making,and create Financial accountability through Collaboration**

### Phases
Three phases of FinOps
- Inform 
- Optimize
- Operate

### Principles
FinOps Principles has six guiding Principles

- Teams need to collaborate
  - Finance, technology, product and business teams work together in near real time as cloud operates on a per-resource, per second basis. work togethers continously to improve and innovate.
- Business value drives technology decisions
  - Unit economics and values based metrics demonstrates business impact better than aggregate spend
  - Make conscious trade-off decision among cost , quality and speed
  - Think of Cloud as driver of Innovation & FinOps as driver of business value
- Everyone takes ownership for their technology usuage
  - accountability of usuage and cost is pushed to edge, Engineers taking ownership of costs from architecture design to ongoing operations
  - Every product or  feature are empowered to manage own usuage against allocated budget
  - Decentralize the decision making around cost-effective architecture, resource usuage and optimization
  - Technical team must consider cost as new efficiency metric from start.
- FinOps data should be accessible , Timely and accurate.
  - Real-time visibility, share process and data ASAP
  - Fast feedback,
  - Create, monitor, and improve Real-time Financial forecasting and planning. 
  - Trending and variance analysis helps expalin why cost increased. 
  - Industry peer-level benchmarking assesses your company's performance.
- FinOps should be enabled centrally
  - Enable best practices in a shared accountability manner.
  - Rate, commitment and discount optimization are centralized.
- Take advantage of the variable cost model of the cloud.
  - Variable cost model shoud be viewed as an oppurtunity to deliver more value, not as a risk. 
  - Emrace in teime predication, planning and purhcasing of capacity
  - Agile iterative planning is preferred over static long term plans
  - Embrace poactive system design with continous adjustment in cloud optimization over infrequent reactive cleanups.
