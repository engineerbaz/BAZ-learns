# FinOps
Collaboration of Technical, Finance, and Business to cater billing/cost of different products and vendors


## FinOps Framework
It enables organizations to **maximize the business value of cloud and Technology, Enable Timely data-driven decision-making,and create Financial accountability through Collaboration**

### Phases
Three phases of FinOps
- Inform 
- Optimize
- Operate

### Principles

- Teams need to collaborate
- Business value drives technology decisions
- Everyone takes ownership for their technology usuage
- FinOps data should be accessible , Timely and accurate.
- FinOps should be enabled centrally
- Take advantage of the variable cost model of the cloud.
